About Us

Laja is India's First forum for women, by women with women working with the single aim of supporting more women and making them become better versions of themselves. We are not a Community or a Society, we are a Forum that strives to make an impact and an improvement in the lives of women around us. LAJA has consistently worked towards the self-development of women and believes that when the woman is secure and aware of her own self, then the home is truly where the heart is. We have monthly skills development meets and that is attended by many women across the country. Our main focus is helping in understanding strengths of women, help them learn skills necessary for upgrading and implementing in their own business . We also focus on mental health and wellness of women. We have multiple activities which help the Laja women to maintain and sustain a healthy mental and emotional self and network with fellow women at a global level to expand their work and business. We have chapters in Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Delhi, Chandigarh, and Hyderabad and each chapter is working towards helping women develop and empower themselves. We are in the process of setting up chapters in two-tier cities as we speak. Our forte is skills development and mental health - counseling and we have helped numerous women across in developing skills-set required for setting up business and process oriented and structured approach is adapted for existing business owners. The founder of LAJA is Ms Riddhi Doshi Patel, Riddhi Doshi Patel is a qualified Child Psychologist, Parenting Counselor , Mental Health practitioner, 3x TEDx Speaker and Advisor to 16 TED-Ed Clubs. She is the recipient of multiple awards including the National Award and Young Entrepreneur Award. She is a well known advocate for Mental Health Awareness and also has the distinction of conducting the very first Online Roundtable Conference to discuss the recommendations of the NEP 2020 and submit a whitepaper on the churnings from the same to The Ministry of Education.
